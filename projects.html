Rebuilding the Lazy River Nursing and Garden Supply Ecommerce Experience

The Problem
Lazy River Nursing and Garden Supply is the largest horticulture supplier in the Northeast with over 50+ million monthly visitors. With growing demand and new entrants on the rise, the organizatin determined that improving it's online store was the only option.

The Action

The approach to designing and developing the site began with reviewing the client's requirements. After conducting thorough reseach, we found that transparency and efficiency were critical to the target audiences flower buying experience, so our team decided to divide the site into three main pages- The Homepage, Currently in Stock, and Hours of Location. Keeping in mind the demographics of our primary users, we used simply HTML text and supporting images on each page to quickly educate users on the offerings.

The Result

The newly developed website took one full day to build. It's new comprehensive design and functionality resulted in a 75% increase in conversion and improved the company's net promoter score by 25%.
